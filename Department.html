Department of Marathi Medium

Department of Marathi Medium was established in the year 1958 .The school has Private building. It has got 20 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 8 boys toilet and it is functional. and 6 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 7760 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 36 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is not having a computer aided learning lab. The school is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal..
