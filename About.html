About Us


                                                                           

Welcome to the dynamic and thriving Gurukul Department at our esteemed New English School, which has been a beacon of education since the inception of the Gurukul program in the academic year 2000-2001. Our department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, boasting a robust Enlish language that supports a conducive learning environment. Embracing modern education methodologies, we conduct engaging digital lectures that foster a comprehensive understanding of Various sub.

Dr. Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil used to travel to various places seeking young talents. He would search them, educate them and help them realize their dreams. This ‘Talent Search’ is carried along in the establishment of Gurukul at Maharaja Sayajirao High School at Satara wherein the selected talented students are being trained and molded. Along with this, non-residential Gurukul Classes are also run at Maharaja Sayajirao Gaikwad and Annasaheb Kalyani High School, Satara, Baramati, Bhuinj, Kaulapur, Dahiwadi and Karad. It is a matter of great pride that every year the students studying in this Gurukul bring laurels by shining in the Merit List of the SSC Board..

We understand that success extends beyond the classroom, which is why our department goes the extra mile. We provide specialized learning programs to prepare students for exam, ensuring they are well-equipped for the professional world. Our commitment to comprehensive education is further underscored by a well-stocked library that serves as a valuable resource for academic pursuits.

In essence, the Gurukul Department at our New English School is not just an academic venture; it is a holistic journey aimed at shaping well-rounded individuals prepared to thrive in the ever-evolving field of computer applications. Join us as we pave the way for a future where knowledge, innovation, and empowerment intersect.
